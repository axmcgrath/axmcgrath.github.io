cosmic pictures

Each quarter links you to a different mode of seeing the cosmos. Experience the universe through the lense of human history, the lense of expensive technologies, or through your own night sky.

Developer's Note: This webpage is designed towards a general audience, to share resources exploring how humans picture outer-space. In keeping with the image theme, I emphasize visual content, creating a symmetric layout with blocked image-links. Since pictures alone are not unambiguous, I have added small captions to appear on mouse-over. I have choosen a neutral/dark color pallete, and used san-serif fonts for a minimalist, mod aesthetic.
