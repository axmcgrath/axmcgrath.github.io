about me

world

My name is Alex. I have lived most of my life near the 45th parallel. I like to explore out from that line.

mind

I can't travel everywhere, so I make up for it in the library. It opens the dimension of time up for travel too. I'm interested in the history of: sovereignty, cartography, drugs, popular justice, colonialism, the environment, globalization, and science, especially astronomy and cosmology. In academics, I've studied the Roman Republic and Early Modern France.

space

I'm also interested in the world outside of earth. Since its even more difficult to get there, I embrace the cosmic aesthetic.
